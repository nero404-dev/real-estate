About Ethan

As a real estate agent, my primary goal is to help clients navigate the exciting and complex world of buying and selling properties. With a deep understanding of the local market and a passion for delivering exceptional service, I am dedicated to ensuring that every client's real estate journey is smooth and successful. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer looking for your dream house or a seasoned investor seeking lucrative opportunities, I am committed to providing personalized guidance and expert advice every step of the way. With my extensive network, negotiation skills, and attention to detail, I strive to make your real estate experience not only rewarding but also enjoyable. Trust me to be your trusted ally in achieving your real estate goals, because your satisfaction is my ultimate priority.
Ethan Fowler is Beirut’s leading apartment sales agent and one of Morton’s leading agents. With over 27 years in real estate and more than 1000 sales, he is renowned for his astute market knowledge and finely honed negotiation skills which enable him to deliver the best prices for his clients. As a Beirut East apartment owner and resident, Ethan brings a patriotic verve and highly specialized knowledge to each of his transactions. His clients identify with his passion and are rewarded with his talent to deliver great results.
